Discover your new destination

Imagine you are Coming home. You take off your shoes and feel better than anywhere else. Immediately you feel the comfort of your rooms and the delicious aroma of the good gastronomy of your kitchen. A unique light like the one of Formentera enters your home through the windows; you sense a feeling of peace like the green trails of Menorca; you treasure vibrant and unrepeatable moments like the ones of Ibiza; you feel fulfilled and happy like in Mallorca. The beautiful mountaint village

A village steeped in old-world charm, Valldemossa lies in an idyllic valley in the midst of the Tramuntana mountains. Its ancient blonde stone houses contrast vividly against the surrounding green forests of olive, oak and almond trees, and the blue sky above. Valldemossa is a very popular tourist destination in the Balearic Islands of Spain

Best views in Palma

Bellver Castle situated just 3km outside of Palma, has towered over the city for over 700 years and is well worth a visit. Claiming the status as the only circular castle in Spain complete with round towers and a moat, the panoramic views from this Gothic treasure across to the magnificent city of Palma in all its glory and the Mediterranean sea beyond are spectacular.

The golden beach

Playa Can Pere Antoni is a long sandy beach enhances the quality of life of its residents and delights its visitors. The beach is located 2km east of the city centre. The golden beach is 750 metres long and 15 metres wide. Although the water isn't as clean as some of Mallorca's more remote bays, it's perfectly adequate for a refreshing swim. Facilities available are sunbed and parasol hire, showers and toilets.
